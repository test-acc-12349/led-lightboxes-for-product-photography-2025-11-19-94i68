About LED Lightboxes

Founded in 2015, LED Lightboxes emerged from a simple vision: to democratize professional product photography for businesses of all sizes. Our journey began when our founder, a passionate photographer frustrated with expensive studio equipment, decided to develop an affordable yet professional-grade lighting solution. Over the past eight years, we've refined our craft, invested in cutting-edge LED technology, and built a reputation for delivering exceptional quality. Today, we serve thousands of e-commerce businesses, photographers, and content creators worldwide, helping them capture stunning product images that drive sales and engagement.

Our mission is to empower creative professionals with accessible, innovative lighting solutions that don't compromise on quality or sustainability. We believe that every business deserves studio-quality product photography, regardless of their budget. We're committed to continuous innovation, environmental responsibility, and customer success. Our core values—quality, accessibility, and sustainability—guide every decision we make, from product design to customer service. We envision a future where professional-grade photography is within reach for everyone, and we're dedicated to making that vision a reality through our products and services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our LED lightboxes and services

Our standard LED lightbox measures 24" x 24" x 24" and weighs just 3.5 pounds, making it incredibly portable. We also offer a larger 36" x 36" x 36" model weighing 6.2 pounds for professional studios. Both models feature collapsible designs that fold flat for easy storage and transportation. The compact dimensions make them perfect for home offices, small studios, or travel photography.

Our premium LED panels are rated for 50,000 hours of continuous operation, which translates to over 5 years of daily use. This far exceeds traditional halogen lighting. While our LEDs are built to last, replacement panels are available and easy to install without any tools. We offer a 3-year warranty on all LED components, and replacement panels are competitively priced at a fraction of the original investment.
